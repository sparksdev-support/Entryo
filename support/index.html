If you need help, have feedback, or want to report an issue, please contact us at: support.coino@gmail.com

Before Contacting Support

When reporting an issue, please include:

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • Your Coino app version
  • A brief description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible

Please do not include sensitive financial information in your email.
