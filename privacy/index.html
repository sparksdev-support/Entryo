Entryo is designed as a privacy-focused personal finance tracker. We do not operate our own servers, and we do not upload your personal finance data to our own servers, sell your personal data, or use it for advertising tracking.

1. Data Stored in the App

Entryo allows you to record personal finance information, such as expenses, income, categories, ledgers, notes, currencies, transaction locations, and app settings.

This information is stored on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, your data may be stored in and synced through your personal iCloud account using Apple’s iCloud services.

We do not have access to your iCloud data.

2. Data collection

Entryo does not collect your personal finance data on our own servers.

We do not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party crash reporting services.

3. Face ID and Touch ID

If you enable app lock, Entryo may use Face ID or Touch ID to unlock the app.

Biometric authentication is handled by iOS. Entryo does not access, collect, store, or transmit your biometric data.

4. Location Permission

If you use the transaction location feature, Entryo may request access to your device location.

Location information is used only to help add or display a location for your transaction record. It is stored with your app data on your device, and may be synced through your personal iCloud account if iCloud sync is enabled.

Entryo does not send your location information to our own servers. Map display, place search, or address lookup may be handled by Apple system services and are subject to Apple’s applicable privacy policies. You can manage location permission at any time in your device settings.

5. In-App Purchases

If Entryo offers in-app purchases, purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

Purchases and refunds are handled by Apple. We do not receive your payment card information.

6. Third-Party Services

Entryo currently does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.

If this changes in the future, this privacy policy will be updated.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your records inside the app. You may also delete the app from your device.

If you use iCloud sync, deleting data from the app may also remove the synced data from iCloud, depending on Apple’s iCloud behavior and your device settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

Entryo is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy when the app changes. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
